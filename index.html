Privacy Policy for Najatok (نَجَاتُك)

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

1. Data Collection & Privacy

No Personal Data Collection: Najatok does not require user accounts. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal identifiable information (PII) such as names, emails, or phone numbers to external servers.

2. Use of Permissions

To provide a full Islamic experience, the app requests the following permissions as defined in our configuration:

  • Notifications: Used solely to send Azkar and prayer reminders at user-defined times.
  • Photo Library: Required only when the user chooses to save a Quranic verse as an image to their device.
  • Microphone: The app does not record audio. This permission is a technical requirement for some background audio playback features (Quran audio).
  • Background Processing: Used to ensure reminders are triggered accurately and to manage local data updates.

3. Data Storage

All user preferences, bookmarks, and progress are stored locally on the device using Hive and SharedPreferences. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app fetches Quranic text and audio from trusted sources (e.g., Quran.com). These requests are for content retrieval only and do not involve user tracking.

سياسة الخصوصية - تطبيق نَجَاتُك

تطبيق نَجَاتُك يحترم خصوصيتك تماماً. نحن لا نقوم بجمع أي بيانات شخصية أو مشاركتها مع أي طرف ثالث.

الأذونات المستخدمة:

  • الإشعارات: لتذكيرك بالأذكار والصلوات.
  • مكتبة الصور: لحفظ صور الآيات القرآنية عند طلبك ذلك.
  • الميكروفون: لا يتم استخدامه للتسجيل، بل هو متطلب تقني لتشغيل الصوت في الخلفية.
